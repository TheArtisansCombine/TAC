Zachariah Perkett

Vocalist • Lyricist • Poet | Upstate NY

Hailing from the quiet backroads and working-class towns of Upstate New York, Zachariah Perkett brings a raw, honest voice to the mic. A vocalist, lyricist, and poet, Zach's work carries the weight of lived experience—equal parts soul, grit, and reflection.

His style blends melodic vocals with poetic storytelling, pulling from the rhythms of daily life, long drives, and late-night thoughts. Whether he’s performing over stripped-back instrumentals or letting his words speak acapella, Zach channels something deeply human—unpolished, real, and resonant.

With roots in small-town stillness and the fire of inner-city edge, Zach is carving out a sound that's all his own. He's currently recording new material and building a body of work that speaks to the struggle, the growth, and the beauty in between.

Stash Spot Ent

Stash Spot Ent is a grassroots music promotion platform based in Western Massachusetts, dedicated to putting Hip Hop and R&B artists center stage. They create opportunities for musicians, vocalists, and performers to connect with live audiences, build their presence, and be part of a thriving Western Mass music scene.

From intimate local showcases to high-energy stage events, Stash Spot Ent curates unforgettable nights where raw talent meets real fans. "Our mission is simple: elevate voices, celebrate culture, and keep the spotlight on the artists shaping our community’s sound." Whether you’re an artist looking to break through or a fan hungry for authentic live performances, Stash Spot Ent is where Western Mass comes together to experience music that moves.

Andy Colon

Founder of Stash Spot Ent

Andy Colon, founder of Stash Spot Ent, is a driving force in Western Mass’s live music scene—bringing Hip Hop & R&B artists to the stage, building community, and creating unforgettable nights where raw talent meets real fans.

Ariandra Lemus

Fashion Designer • Hairdresser • DIY Creator

Ari is a passionate creator whose artistry spans the realms of fashion design, hairdressing, and DIY projects. She finds immense joy in transforming raw materials into beautiful, tangible creations and believes in the power of art to bring people together. Her work, whether on the runway, in the salon chair, or crafted by hand, is a testament to her dedication to artistic growth and her ability to elevate ideas from initial concept to stunning realities that connect and inspire.

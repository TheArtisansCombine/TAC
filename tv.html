Welcome to TAC TV, your window into the dynamic visual arts scene of Western Massachusetts. Here, we showcase a curated selection of **short films, artist documentaries, performance recordings, and creative narratives** that capture the essence of our vibrant community.

Explore the stories behind the art, witness captivating live performances, and gain insights into the creative processes of local filmmakers, dancers, musicians, and visual artists. From experimental shorts to compelling interviews, TAC TV is dedicated to bringing the rich visual tapestry of our region directly to you.

Immerse yourself in the talent that thrives in our valleys and hills. New content is added regularly, so keep an eye out for fresh perspectives and inspiring visual journeys.

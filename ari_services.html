Explore Ari's Creative Ventures

Dive deeper into the world of Ari's unique talents in hair artistry and fashion design.

GetFabb with Ari

As a dedicated and skilled hairdresser, Ari offers a full spectrum of salon services aimed at bringing your hair dreams to life. From precision cuts and vibrant coloring to intricate styling for special occasions, Ari approaches each client with a personalized touch. She believes a great hairstyle isn't just about technique; it's about understanding your unique personality and lifestyle to create a look that empowers you. Step into her chair and experience a transformation that enhances your natural beauty and boosts your confidence.

Ari Jays Designs

Ari Jays Designs is the creative hub where Ari's passion for fashion and DIY artistry truly flourishes. This is a space dedicated to innovative fashion concepts, from avant-garde pieces to custom-tailored creations that reflect individuality. Beyond clothing, Ari transforms everyday materials into extraordinary art pieces through her diverse DIY projects, showcasing sustainable practices and imaginative craftsmanship. Whether it's a unique garment or a handcrafted decor item, each design tells a story of creativity, skill, and a deep love for making. Explore her portfolio to see how she blends aesthetics with bespoke artistry, pushing the boundaries of traditional design.

Meet Ariandra Lemus

Ariandra Lemus is the creative force behind both GetFabb with Ari and Ari Jays Designs, bringing her passion for hairdressing, fashion, and DIY artistry to life.

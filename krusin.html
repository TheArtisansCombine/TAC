Krusin B Krusin

Independent Music Producer & Artist

Based in Springfield, Massachusetts, Krusin is an independent producer and recording artist blending multi-genre sounds with a theatrical and instrumental background. Through Krusin Music / SaySome Studios, Krusin creates original tracks, remixes, and collaborations. Krusin's style fuses hip-hop, experimental, and eclectic influences, delivering music that’s energetic, creative, and unapologetically unique.

Krusin's Musical Journey

Krusin's musical journey is deeply rooted in the art of songwriting and instrumental creation. Krusin meticulously crafts compelling narratives through his lyrics and builds immersive soundscapes with his original instrumentals. A significant part of his collaborative spirit shines through his work with fellow artist Kaine Marqo, with whom Krusin has embarked on an exciting new project called The Wav Welders. This collaboration explores new sonic territories, pushing creative boundaries and delivering fresh, innovative music.

Beyond his personal projects, Krusin extends his expertise to other artists by offering his services as a male vocalist/rapper on Fiverr. Krusin provides professional and discreet services for crafting impactful verses and hooks, helping fellow musicians bring their lyrical visions to life. This service allows Krusin to connect with and contribute to a wider musical community, providing high-quality, custom-written content tailored to diverse artistic needs.

Jesus Alicea

Curator, Artist, and Marketer

A multidisciplinary professional from Western Massachusetts, Jesus is the curator and mind behind The Artisans Combine. By day, he works in sales and marketing with MVP Marketing Results, and by night, he is a passionate artist at Alicea Media Arts.

Alicea Media Arts

Alicea Media Arts is where I channel my passion for creative storytelling. It's a multidisciplinary studio blending visual design and language to produce work that's both expressive and functional. From logos and posters to poetry and character-driven advertising cartoons, every piece is crafted to connect, communicate, and inspire.

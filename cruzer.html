Cruzer Urameshi

Independent Music Producer | Streamer | Visionary

Born in Columbus, Georgia and raised just outside Atlanta, Cruzer Urameshi grew up surrounded by music, anime, and retro video games — influences that still shape his signature sound today. After moving to Oregon right out of high school, he built his career independently, turning late-night ideas into tracks that feel like cinematic adventures.

Cruzer's Music

Cruzer Urameshi is a boundary-pushing producer blending hip-hop, lofi, synth-wave, and ambient styles into immersive sonic worlds. Starting at just 16, he’s collaborated with names like Shoreline Mafia, CheckTheStar, HotBoy Turk, and Famous Kid Brick, while also crafting standout solo projects such as Cruzer 64, One Nice Rain, and the Synergy EP — home to the viral hit “Synth Wave” with 28M+ views. His work is featured on Apple Music, Spotify, and TIDAL, connecting with listeners worldwide through an independent vision and a sound that’s both nostalgic and futuristic.

His passion for lifting others up is as strong as his drive to create.

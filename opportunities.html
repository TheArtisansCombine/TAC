ARToberFEST Westfield 2025

Deadline: September 13, 2025 | Event: September 27, 2025

Deadline: August 1, 2025

Grants of $1,500-$5,000 to USA writers and translators for literary projects (memoirs, novels, essays, poetry, etc.).

Submit artwork or poetry for their 55th annual wall calendar celebrating peace, justice, and hope. Location: Syracuse, NY.
